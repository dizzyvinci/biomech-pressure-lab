🔗 **Kinetic chain (it's all connected to the foot):** high landing force -> ACL
   signature: _steep dF/dt at landing (stiff)_ → ACL, tibial bone stress

⚠️ **Injury-relevant overload at met2, met3** — hallux (turf toe / sesamoid) + central-met; wooden court raises hallux vs asphalt
→ Load has shifted **off the hallux onto the central met heads** — the published 1st-MTP-pain pattern; central-met (2nd-met) overload is the 'dancer's fracture' route. Cue big-toe engagement / check the 1st ray.
